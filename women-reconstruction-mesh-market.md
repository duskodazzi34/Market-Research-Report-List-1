<p><p>The Women's Reconstruction Mesh Market includes various surgical approaches for pelvic floor repair. The **Transobturator Approach** uses a mesh inserted through the inner thigh for minimal invasiveness. The **Vaginal Approach** involves direct access through the vaginal canal, while the **Laparoscopic Approach** utilizes small incisions and a camera for precision. The **Retropubic Approach** accesses the pelvic area through the abdominal wall, contrasting with the **Suprapubic Approach**, which enters above the pubic bone. The **Transgluteal Approach** navigates through the gluteal muscles. Additional methods fall under "Other" categories.</p></p>
