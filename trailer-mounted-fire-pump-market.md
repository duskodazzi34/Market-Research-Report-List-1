<p><p>The trailer-mounted fire pump market can be categorized into two main types: single-stage and two-stage centrifugal fire pumps. Single-stage centrifugal fire pumps are designed for moderate pressure applications, providing efficient water flow for firefighting needs. In contrast, two-stage centrifugal fire pumps offer higher pressure capabilities, making them suitable for more demanding situations, such as combating large-scale fires or reaching elevated areas. Both types are mounted on trailers for mobility and ease of deployment in emergency scenarios.</p></p>
