<p><p>A Liquid Electrostatic Spray Gun is a specialized painting tool that utilizes electrostatic principles to apply coatings, enhancing efficiency and finish quality. This technology is particularly favored in industries such as automotive, aerospace, and furniture manufacturing, where high-quality finishes are essential.</p><p>The Liquid Electrostatic Spray Gun Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 8.7% during the forecast period.
