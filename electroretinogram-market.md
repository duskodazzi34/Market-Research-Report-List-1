<p><p>An Electroretinogram (ERG) is a diagnostic test that measures the electrical responses of the retina to light stimulation. It is crucial for assessing retinal function, diagnosing conditions like retinitis pigmentosa, and evaluating other ocular diseases. The ERG process involves placing electrodes on the cornea or skin surrounding the eye to record the electrical activity generated by the photoreceptors.</p><p>The Electroretinogram Market is witnessing significant growth, driven by advancements in diagnostic technologies, increasing prevalence of retinal disorders, and a rising geriatric population susceptible to these conditions. Additionally, awareness regarding the importance of early detection and diagnosis of eye diseases is propelling market demand. </p><p>Latest trends in the market include the integration of digital technologies, miniaturized devices for easier use, and telemedicine solutions that enhance accessibility to ERG testing. The growing emphasis on personalized medicine also influences market dynamics as tailored therapeutic approaches emerge for retinal conditions.
