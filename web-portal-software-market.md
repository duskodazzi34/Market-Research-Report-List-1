<p><p>Web portal software is available in two primary market types: on-premise and cloud-based. On-premise solutions are installed directly on an organization's servers, offering greater control and customization while requiring significant upfront investment in infrastructure and maintenance. In contrast, cloud-based portals are hosted on remote servers, providing easy accessibility, scalability, and lower initial costs. These solutions allow businesses to access the software from anywhere, facilitating collaboration and reducing the burden on internal IT resources. Each type caters to different organizational needs.</p></p>
