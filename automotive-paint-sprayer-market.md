<p><p>The automotive paint sprayer market comprises several types, primarily High Volume Low Pressure (HVLP) and Low Volume Low Pressure (LVLP) sprayers. HVLP sprayers use a higher volume of air at lower pressure, resulting in less overspray and better material transfer, making them ideal for detailed work. LVLP sprayers, on the other hand, operate at lower air volume and pressure, offering improved control for smaller projects. Other types may include airless and electrostatic sprayers, catering to various application needs and efficiencies.</p></p>
