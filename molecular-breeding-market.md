<p><p>Molecular breeding involves various techniques to enhance crop traits through genetic analyses. The Single Nucleotide Polymorphism (SNP) market focuses on identifying variations at a single DNA position, facilitating precise trait selection and faster breeding cycles. Simple Sequence Repeats (SSR) involve repetitive DNA sequences that help assess genetic diversity and markers for breeding. The “Others” market encompasses additional molecular techniques, such as genotyping-by-sequencing and genome-wide association studies, that support advanced breeding strategies for improving agricultural productivity.</p></p>
