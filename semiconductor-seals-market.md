<p><p>The semiconductor seals market encompasses various types, including FKM (Fluoroelastomer), FFKM (Perfluoroelastomer), and Fluorosilicone. FKM seals offer excellent chemical resistance and high-temperature stability, ideal for aggressive environments. FFKM seals provide superior performance in extreme conditions, resisting harsh chemicals and maintaining integrity over time. Fluorosilicone combines the properties of silicone and fluorocarbon, making it suitable for applications involving both high temperatures and potential chemical exposure. Other seals may include materials tailored for specific applications or environments within the semiconductor industry.</p></p>
