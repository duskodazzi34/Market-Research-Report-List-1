<p><p>Light Guide Plates (LGP) are essential components in LCD TVs, enhancing backlighting and uniformity. The market is divided into two types: Printing Type and Print-less Type. Printing Type LGPs use various printing techniques to create patterns that control light diffusion, optimizing brightness and contrast. In contrast, Print-less Type LGPs rely on advanced manufacturing processes, such as laser etching or molding, to achieve light guidance without traditional printing, leading to a more streamlined production and potentially lower costs. Both types aim to improve visual performance in TVs.</p></p>
