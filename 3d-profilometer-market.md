<p><ul><li>"Contact"</li><li>"Non-contact"</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The 3D profilometer market is divided into two main types: contact and non-contact. </p><p>Contact profilometers utilize mechanical stylus probes to physically touch and measure surface profiles, providing high accuracy but potentially damaging delicate surfaces. Non-contact profilometers, on the other hand, employ optical or laser techniques to capture surface data without physical interaction, making them suitable for fragile materials and providing faster measurements. Both types cater to diverse industries, including manufacturing, automotive, and electronics, with specific applications determined by their measurement requirements.</p></p>
