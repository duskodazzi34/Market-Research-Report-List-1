<p><p>The automatic pipetting workstation market is primarily divided into two types: single-channel and multi-channel automated pipetting workstations. Single-channel automated pipetting workstations are designed for precise, one-at-a-time liquid handling, ideal for workflows requiring high accuracy, such as in genomics and proteomics. In contrast, multi-channel automated pipetting workstations enable simultaneous handling of multiple samples, significantly enhancing throughput and efficiency in applications like drug discovery and high-throughput screening. Both types improve reliability and reduce human error in laboratory environments.</p></p>
