<p><p>The metalworking tools market encompasses various types essential for shaping and machining metal components. **Turning tools** are used for cutting cylindrical parts on lathes, while **milling tools** create complex shapes and features through rotational motion. **Drilling tools** produce holes in materials, crucial for assembly and fastening. **Threading tools** are specialized for creating screw threads, allowing for secure connections. The **others** category includes additional tools like grinding and boring tools, which enhance precision and finish in metalworking processes.</p></p>
