<p><p>The Train Protection and Warning System (TPWS) enhances railway safety by preventing collisions and protecting trains from exceeding speed limits. It consists of three market types: Automatic Train Protection (ATP), which autonomously controls train speeds based on signals; Automatic Train Operation (ATO), enabling trains to operate with minimal human intervention; and Automatic Train Supervision (ATS), which oversees train movements and schedules. Together, these systems ensure safe and efficient railway operations, minimizing human error and enhancing overall network reliability.</p></p>
