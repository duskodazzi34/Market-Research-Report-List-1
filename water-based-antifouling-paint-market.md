<p><strong>The Water-based Antifouling Paint Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Self Polishing</li><li>Controlled Release</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The water-based antifouling paint market comprises primarily two types: self-polishing and controlled release. Self-polishing antifouling paints work by gradually eroding their surface, continually exposing fresh biocides to inhibit the growth of marine organisms. In contrast, controlled release antifouling paints utilize a more precise method of biocide release, ensuring a consistent and long-lasting effect against fouling organisms. Both types aim to enhance vessel performance while minimizing environmental impacts, making them essential for marine applications.</p></p>
