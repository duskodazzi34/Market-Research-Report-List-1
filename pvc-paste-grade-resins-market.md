<p><strong>The PVC Paste Grade Resins Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Micro Suspension Method</li><li>Emulsion Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>PVC paste grade resins are produced using two main methods: the micro suspension method and the emulsion method. </p><p>The micro suspension method involves dispersing PVC resin particles in a liquid medium, resulting in fine particles with improved flow and texture, ideal for applications requiring smooth finishes. </p><p>The emulsion method, on the other hand, entails creating a stable mixture of PVC resin in water with surfactants, leading to resins with excellent processing characteristics and lower viscosity. Both methods contribute to versatile applications in coatings and adhesives.</p></p>
