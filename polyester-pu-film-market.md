<p><p>The Polyester PU Film market consists of two main types: Pure-Polyester and Polyether-Polyester. Pure-Polyester films are known for their excellent mechanical properties, chemical resistance, and durability, making them suitable for various industrial applications. In contrast, Polyether-Polyester films offer enhanced flexibility and hydrolytic stability, making them ideal for applications that require moisture resistance and improved elongation. Both types cater to diverse demands in sectors like automotive, textiles, and packaging, leveraging their unique characteristics for tailored performance.</p></p>
