<p><p>Lightweight Expanded Clay Aggregate (LECA) is a specialized type of aggregate made from clay that is expanded through high-temperature processing. LECA is increasingly used in lightweight concrete, floor systems, and green roofs due to its low density and effective drainage properties.</p><p>The Lightweight Expanded Clay Aggregate (LECA) Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 11.2% during the forecast period. Emerging trends in the market involve the incorporation of LECA in innovative building applications, such as vertical gardens and geotechnical projects. Furthermore, advancements in manufacturing processes aim to enhance the quality and performance of LECA products, further promoting their adoption.
