<p><p>The AT torque converter market is divided into single-stage and multistage torque converters. In contrast, multistage torque converters feature multiple turbine stages that enhance efficiency and performance, enabling smoother acceleration and improved fuel economy. Both types are essential components in automatic transmissions, catering to diverse vehicle performance needs and driving conditions, thus influencing their market dynamics and growth potential.</p></p>
