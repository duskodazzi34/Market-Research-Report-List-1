<p><ul><li>Release Liner Labels</li><li>Linerless Labels</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pressure labels market primarily includes two types: release liner labels and linerless labels. Release liner labels feature a backing material that provides easy peeling off for application, ensuring efficient labeling. They are commonly used in various industries for product branding and identification. Conversely, linerless labels eliminate the backing, allowing for more label material on a roll and reducing waste. This innovation enhances sustainability and operational efficiency, making linerless labels increasingly popular for applications requiring streamlined labeling solutions.</p></p>
