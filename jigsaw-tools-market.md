<p><ul><li>Straight Reciprocating Jigsaw</li><li>Orbital Reciprocating Jigsaw</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The jigsaw tools market is primarily segmented into two main types: straight reciprocating jigsaws and orbital reciprocating jigsaws. Straight reciprocating jigsaws offer precise cuts in a straight path, ideal for intricate and detailed work, such as crafting and fine woodworking. In contrast, orbital reciprocating jigsaws feature a pendulum motion, allowing for faster, more aggressive cutting through thicker materials like wood and metal. Each type caters to different user needs, making them essential for professionals and DIY enthusiasts alike.</p></p>
