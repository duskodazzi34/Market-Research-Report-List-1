<p><strong>The UV-curable Polyurethane Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Component</li><li>Two-component</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The UV-curable polyurethane market is categorized into single-component and two-component systems. Single-component systems contain all necessary components in one package, requiring only UV exposure for curing, simplifying application and storage. In contrast, two-component systems involve a separate hardener that mixes with the resin before exposure to UV light, offering enhanced chemical resistance and performance but requiring careful handling. Both types cater to various applications, including coatings, adhesives, and sealants, driven by their quick curing times and environmental benefits.</p></p>
