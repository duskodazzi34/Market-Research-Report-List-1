<p><p>The night vision camera market is primarily segmented into fixed cameras and PTZ (pan-tilt-zoom) cameras. Fixed cameras are stationary devices that provide a constant view of a specific area, making them ideal for monitoring entrances and perimeters. In contrast, PTZ cameras offer flexibility, allowing operators to remotely pan, tilt, and zoom to cover larger areas or track moving subjects. Both types are essential for security and surveillance in low-light conditions, catering to various user needs across different environments.</p></p>
