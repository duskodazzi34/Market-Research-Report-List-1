<p><p>The automotive laminating adhesives market comprises several types: solventborne, solventless, waterborne, and others. Solventborne adhesives contain volatile organic compounds, providing strong bonding yet posing environmental concerns. Solventless adhesives eliminate solvents, offering a cleaner application with minimal emissions. Waterborne adhesives utilize water as a carrier, resulting in lower VOCs and improved safety. Other types may include hot melt and reactive adhesives, catering to specific applications and materials. Together, these types address diverse bonding needs in the automotive industry.</p></p>
