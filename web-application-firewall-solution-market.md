<p><p>The Web Application Firewall (WAF) market includes two primary types: On-Premises WAF Solutions and Cloud-Based WAF Solutions. On-Premises WAFs are deployed within a client’s local infrastructure, offering complete control and customization, often requiring significant IT resources for management. In contrast, Cloud-Based WAFs are hosted by third-party providers, offering easier deployment, scalability, and maintenance, often featuring real-time updates and threat intelligence. Both solutions aim to protect web applications by filtering and monitoring HTTP traffic for vulnerabilities and attacks.</p></p>
