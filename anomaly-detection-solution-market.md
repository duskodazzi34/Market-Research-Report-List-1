<p><p>Anomaly detection solutions encompass various market types, notably Network Behavior Anomaly Detection and User Behavior Anomaly Detection. Network Behavior Anomaly Detection focuses on identifying irregular patterns in network traffic that may signal potential security threats or breaches. In contrast, User Behavior Anomaly Detection analyzes individual user activities, aiming to detect deviations from established behavior patterns that could indicate compromised accounts or insider threats. Both approaches are essential for enhancing cybersecurity and ensuring the integrity of IT systems within organizations.</p></p>
