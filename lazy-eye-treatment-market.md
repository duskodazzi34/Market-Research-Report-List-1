<p><ul><li>Strabismic Lazy Eye</li><li>Refractive Lazy Eye</li><li>Deprivation Lazy Eye</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Lazy Eye Treatment Market is segmented into three main types: Strabismic Lazy Eye, Refractive Lazy Eye, and Deprivation Lazy Eye. Strabismic Lazy Eye arises from misalignment of the eyes, requiring corrective measures like surgery or vision therapy. Refractive Lazy Eye involves unbalanced refractive errors, treated primarily through corrective lenses or vision training. Deprivation Lazy Eye is caused by vision obstruction, often addressed with patching, surgery, or optical correction. Each type necessitates tailored treatment for optimal vision restoration.</p></p>
