<p><strong>The Lateral Flow Rapid Testing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Sandwich Immunoassay</li><li>Competitive Immunoassay</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Lateral Flow Rapid Testing market primarily includes two types: Sandwich Immunoassay and Competitive Immunoassay. </p><p>Sandwich Immunoassays utilize two antibodies for detecting the target analyte, enhancing sensitivity and specificity. This method is commonly employed for measuring biomarkers and pathogens, making it suitable for various diagnostic applications. </p><p>Competitive Immunoassays, on the other hand, involve competition between the target analyte and a labeled analog for binding to a specific antibody. This approach is often used for small molecules or low-abundance targets, providing a versatile testing option.</p></p>
