<p><p>The pharmaceutical grade sorbitol market encompasses two main types: sorbitol solution and sorbitol crystals. Sorbitol solution is a clear, viscous liquid used as a humectant, sweetener, and stabilizer in various pharmaceutical formulations. It enhances texture and moisture retention. In contrast, sorbitol crystals are solid and often utilized as a bulking agent and sweetener in tablet formulations and other solid dosage forms. Both types are prized for their safety, low-caloric sweetness, and versatile applicability in drug manufacturing.</p></p>
