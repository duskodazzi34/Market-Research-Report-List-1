<p><p>Polyvinylidene Fluoride (PVDF) is a highly non-reactive and pure thermoplastic fluoropolymer, known for its excellent chemical resistance, low friction properties, and durability. The PVDF market can be segmented into two main types: PVDF granule and PVDF powder. PVDF granules are typically used in applications requiring molding and extrusion, providing robust mechanical properties. In contrast, PVDF powder is ideal for coatings and specialized applications, offering flexibility in formulation and enabling fine surface finishes. Both forms cater to diverse industrial applications.</p></p>
