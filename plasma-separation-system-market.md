<p><p>The Plasma Separation System Market comprises two main types: membrane separation and centrifugal separation. Membrane separation utilizes selective membranes to separate plasma components based on size and charge, offering efficient and scalable processes. In contrast, centrifugal separation employs high-speed rotation to create a centrifugal force that separates plasma from other blood components by density. Both methods are crucial for providing high-purity plasma for medical applications, with each type having distinct advantages in terms of efficiency, cost, and application suitability.</p></p>
